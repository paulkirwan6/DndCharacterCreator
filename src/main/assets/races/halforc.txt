HALF-ORC

Ability Score Increase: +2 Strength, +1 Constitution

Age: Mature faster than humans, reach adulthood around 14, live to about 75 years

Size: Medium (6-7 feet tall)

Speed: 30 feet

Languages: Common and Orc

Traits:
• Darkvision: See in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light
• Menacing: Proficiency in Intimidation skill
• Relentless Endurance: When reduced to 0 HP but not killed, drop to 1 HP instead (once per long rest)
• Savage Attacks: When you score a critical hit with melee weapon, roll one additional weapon damage die
• Excellent choice for martial classes, especially Barbarian and Fighter
