GNOME

Ability Score Increase: +2 Intelligence

Age: Mature around 40, live 350-500 years

Size: Small (3-4 feet tall)

Speed: 25 feet

Languages: Common and Gnomish

Traits:
• Darkvision: See in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light
• Gnome Cunning: Advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saves against magic

SUBRACES:

Forest Gnome
• +1 Dexterity
• Natural Illusionist: Know the minor illusion cantrip (Intelligence is spellcasting ability)
• Speak with Small Beasts: Communicate simple ideas with Small or smaller beasts

Rock Gnome
• +1 Constitution
• Artificer's Lore: Add double proficiency bonus to Intelligence (History) checks for magic items, alchemical objects, or technological devices
• Tinker: Proficiency with artisan's tools (tinker's tools). Can create tiny clockwork devices
