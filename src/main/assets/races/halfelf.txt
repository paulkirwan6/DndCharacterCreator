HALF-ELF

Ability Score Increase: +2 Charisma, +1 to two other ability scores of your choice

Age: Mature at same rate as humans, live about 180 years

Size: Medium (5-6 feet tall)

Speed: 30 feet

Languages: Common, Elvish, and one extra language of your choice

Traits:
• Darkvision: See in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light
• Fey Ancestry: Advantage on saves against being charmed, magic can't put you to sleep
• Skill Versatility: Proficiency in two skills of your choice
• Combines human versatility with elven grace
• Excellent choice for Charisma-based classes like Bard, Paladin, Sorcerer, or Warlock
