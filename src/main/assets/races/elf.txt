ELF

Ability Score Increase: +2 Dexterity

Age: Reach adulthood around 100, live up to 750 years

Size: Medium (5-6 feet tall, slender build)

Speed: 30 feet

Languages: Common and Elvish

Traits:
• Darkvision: See in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light
• Keen Senses: Proficiency in Perception skill
• Fey Ancestry: Advantage on saves against being charmed, magic can't put you to sleep
• Trance: Meditate 4 hours instead of sleeping 8 hours

SUBRACES:

High Elf
• +1 Intelligence
• Weapon Training: Proficiency with longsword, shortsword, shortbow, and longbow
• Cantrip: Know one wizard cantrip of your choice
• Extra Language: Learn one additional language

Wood Elf
• +1 Wisdom
• Weapon Training: Proficiency with longsword, shortsword, shortbow, and longbow
• Fleet of Foot: Base walking speed increases to 35 feet
• Mask of the Wild: Can attempt to hide in light foliage, rain, snow, or mist

Drow (Dark Elf)
• +1 Charisma
• Superior Darkvision: Darkvision extends to 120 feet
• Sunlight Sensitivity: Disadvantage on attack rolls and Perception checks in sunlight
• Drow Magic: Know dancing lights cantrip. At 3rd level, cast faerie fire once per day. At 5th level, cast darkness once per day
• Weapon Training: Proficiency with rapiers, shortswords, and hand crossbows
