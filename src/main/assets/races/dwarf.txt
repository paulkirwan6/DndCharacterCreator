DWARF

Ability Score Increase: +2 Constitution

Age: Mature around 50, live about 350 years

Size: Medium (4-5 feet tall, stocky and heavy)

Speed: 25 feet (not reduced by wearing heavy armor)

Languages: Common and Dwarvish

Traits:
• Darkvision: See in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light
• Dwarven Resilience: Advantage on saves against poison, resistance to poison damage
• Dwarven Combat Training: Proficiency with battleaxe, handaxe, light hammer, and warhammer
• Tool Proficiency: Proficiency with artisan's tools (smith's, brewer's, or mason's)
• Stonecunning: Add double proficiency bonus to Intelligence (History) checks related to stonework

SUBRACES:

Hill Dwarf
• +1 Wisdom
• Dwarven Toughness: Hit point maximum increases by 1 per level

Mountain Dwarf
• +2 Strength
• Armor Training: Proficiency with light and medium armor
