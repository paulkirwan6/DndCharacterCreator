DRAGONBORN

Ability Score Increase: +2 Strength, +1 Charisma

Age: Reach adulthood by 15, live to about 80 years

Size: Medium (over 6 feet tall, 250+ pounds)

Speed: 30 feet

Languages: Common and Draconic

Traits:
• Draconic Ancestry: Choose one dragon type, determines breath weapon and damage resistance
  - Black (Acid), Blue (Lightning), Brass (Fire), Bronze (Lightning), Copper (Acid)
  - Gold (Fire), Green (Poison), Red (Fire), Silver (Cold), White (Cold)
• Breath Weapon: Exhale destructive energy based on ancestry
  - 5 by 30 ft. line (Black, Blue, Brass, Bronze, Copper) or 15 ft. cone (Gold, Green, Red, Silver, White)
  - Damage: 2d6 at 1st level, increases at higher levels
  - DC: 8 + Constitution modifier + proficiency bonus
  - Usable once per short or long rest
• Damage Resistance: Resistance to damage type associated with draconic ancestry
