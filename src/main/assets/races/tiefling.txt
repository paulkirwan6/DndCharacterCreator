TIEFLING

Ability Score Increase: +2 Charisma, +1 Intelligence

Age: Mature at same rate as humans, live a few years longer

Size: Medium (same size and build as humans)

Speed: 30 feet

Languages: Common and Infernal

Traits:
• Darkvision: See in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light
• Hellish Resistance: Resistance to fire damage
• Infernal Legacy: Innate spellcasting (Charisma is spellcasting ability)
  - Know thaumaturgy cantrip
  - At 3rd level: Cast hellish rebuke once per long rest as 2nd-level spell
  - At 5th level: Cast darkness once per long rest
• Infernal heritage makes them natural spellcasters
• Great choice for Warlock, Sorcerer, Bard, or Paladin
