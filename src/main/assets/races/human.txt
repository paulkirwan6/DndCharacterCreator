HUMAN

Ability Score Increase: +1 to all ability scores

Age: Reach adulthood in late teens, live less than a century

Size: Medium (5-6 feet tall)

Speed: 30 feet

Languages: Common and one extra language of your choice

Traits:
• Versatile and adaptable to any class or role
• No specific racial traits, but the ability score bonus applies to everything
• Extra language provides flexibility for roleplay and communication
• Most common and diverse race in most D&D worlds
• Excellent choice for any class due to balanced ability score increases
