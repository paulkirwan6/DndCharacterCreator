COLLEGE OF LORE (Bard Subclass)

Available at 3rd level

Bards of the College of Lore are masters of knowledge and secrets, using their wit and skill to support allies and confound enemies.

FEATURES:

Bonus Proficiencies (3rd level)
• Gain proficiency with three skills of your choice

Cutting Words (3rd level)
• Use your reaction to expend one Bardic Inspiration die
• Roll the die and subtract the number from a creature's attack roll, ability check, or damage roll
• You can use this after the creature makes its roll but before the DM determines success or failure
• The creature must be within 60 feet and able to hear you

Additional Magical Secrets (6th level)
• Learn two spells of your choice from any class
• The spells count as bard spells for you but don't count against your number of bard spells known
• The spells must be of a level you can cast

Peerless Skill (14th level)
• When you make an ability check, you can expend one use of Bardic Inspiration
• Roll the Bardic Inspiration die and add it to your ability check
• You can choose to do so after you roll the die for the ability check, but before the DM tells you whether you succeed or fail
