CIRCLE OF THE MOON (Druid Subclass)

Available at 2nd level

Druids of the Circle of the Moon are fierce guardians of the wilds who take on powerful animal forms in combat.

FEATURES:

Combat Wild Shape (2nd level)
• You can use Wild Shape as a bonus action instead of as an action
• While in Wild Shape, you can expend one spell slot to regain 1d8 hit points per level of the spell slot expended

Circle Forms (2nd level)
• You can transform into beasts with a challenge rating as high as 1 (ignoring the Max CR column of the Beast Shapes table)
• Starting at 6th level, you can transform into a beast with a challenge rating as high as your druid level divided by 3, rounded down
• Example CR progression: 2nd level (CR 1), 6th level (CR 2), 9th level (CR 3), 12th level (CR 4), 15th level (CR 5), 18th level (CR 6)

Primal Strike (6th level)
• Your attacks in beast form count as magical for the purpose of overcoming resistance and immunity to nonmagical attacks and damage

Elemental Wild Shape (10th level)
• You can expend two uses of Wild Shape at the same time to transform into an air elemental, earth elemental, fire elemental, or water elemental

Thousand Forms (14th level)
• You have learned to use magic to alter your physical form in more subtle ways
• You can cast the alter self spell at will
