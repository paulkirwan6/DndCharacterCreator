ELDRITCH KNIGHT (Fighter Subclass)

Available at 3rd level

Eldritch Knights combine martial prowess with the study of magic, using spells to supplement their combat abilities.

SPELLCASTING:
• Spell Slots: Gain spell slots as you level (1/3 caster progression)
• Spells Known: Learn a limited number of wizard spells
• Spellcasting Ability: Intelligence
• Spell Focus: Can use weapon as spellcasting focus

SPELL RESTRICTIONS:
• Most spells must be from the abjuration or evocation schools
• At 3rd, 8th, 14th, and 20th level, you can learn a spell from any school

FEATURES:

Spellcasting (3rd level)
• Learn three wizard cantrips
• Learn three 1st-level wizard spells (two must be abjuration or evocation)
• Gain spell slots and learn more spells as you level up

Weapon Bond (3rd level)
• Perform a 1-hour ritual to bond with one or two weapons
• You can't be disarmed of a bonded weapon unless you are incapacitated
• If a bonded weapon is on the same plane of existence, you can summon it as a bonus action, causing it to teleport instantly to your hand

War Magic (7th level)
• When you use your action to cast a cantrip, you can make one weapon attack as a bonus action

Eldritch Strike (10th level)
• When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, that creature has disadvantage on the next saving throw it makes against a spell you cast before the end of your next turn

Arcane Charge (15th level)
• When you use your Action Surge, you can teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see
• You can teleport before or after the additional action

Improved War Magic (18th level)
• When you use your action to cast a spell, you can make one weapon attack as a bonus action
