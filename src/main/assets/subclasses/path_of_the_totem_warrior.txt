PATH OF THE TOTEM WARRIOR (Barbarian Subclass)

Available at 3rd level

Totem warriors draw spiritual power from animal spirits, gaining supernatural abilities that reflect their chosen totem.

FEATURES:

Spirit Seeker (3rd level)
• Gain the ability to cast beast sense and speak with animals as rituals

Totem Spirit (3rd level)
Choose one totem animal. Gain its benefit while raging:

Bear
• Resistance to all damage except psychic damage
• Most popular choice for maximum survivability

Eagle
• Other creatures have disadvantage on opportunity attack rolls against you
• You can use the Dash action as a bonus action

Wolf
• Your friends have advantage on melee attack rolls against any creature within 5 feet of you that is hostile to you

Aspect of the Beast (6th level)
Choose one totem animal:

Bear
• Carrying capacity doubles
• Advantage on Strength checks to push, pull, lift, or break objects

Eagle
• You can see up to 1 mile away with no difficulty
• Dim light doesn't impose disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks

Wolf
• You can track creatures while traveling at a fast pace
• You can move stealthily while traveling at a normal pace

Spirit Walker (10th level)
• Cast commune with nature as a ritual
• When you do so, a spiritual version of your totem animal appears to convey information

Totemic Attunement (14th level)
Choose one totem animal:

Bear
• While raging, any creature within 5 feet that's hostile to you has disadvantage on attack rolls against targets other than you or another Totem Warrior with this feature
• Enemy must be able to see or hear you and not be incapacitated

Eagle
• While raging, you have a flying speed equal to your current walking speed
• This benefit works only in short bursts; you fall if you end your turn in the air

Wolf
• While raging, you can use a bonus action on your turn to knock a Large or smaller creature prone when you hit it with a melee weapon attack
