BATTLE MASTER (Fighter Subclass)

Available at 3rd level

The Battle Master is a tactical fighter who uses superiority dice to perform special combat maneuvers.

FEATURES:

Combat Superiority (3rd level)
• Gain 4 superiority dice (d8)
• Learn 3 maneuvers from the Battle Master list
• Regain all superiority dice on short or long rest

Student of War (3rd level)
• Gain proficiency with one type of artisan's tools

Know Your Enemy (7th level)
• Spend 1 minute observing a creature to learn if it's equal, superior, or inferior to you in two characteristics: Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Armor Class, Current HP, Total Class Levels, or Fighter Class Levels

Improved Combat Superiority (10th level)
• Superiority dice become d10

Relentless (15th level)
• Regain 1 superiority die if you have none at the start of your turn

Additional Superiority Dice (18th level)
• Superiority dice become d12
• Gain 2 more superiority dice (total of 6)

MANEUVERS (Choose 3 at 3rd level, more at higher levels):
• Commander's Strike: Direct ally to attack
• Disarming Attack: Force enemy to drop item
• Distracting Strike: Give ally advantage
• Evasive Footwork: Increase AC while moving
• Feinting Attack: Gain advantage on attack
• Goading Attack: Provoke enemy to attack you
• Lunging Attack: Increase reach by 5 feet
• Maneuvering Attack: Allow ally to move without provoking
• Menacing Attack: Frighten enemy
• Parry: Reduce incoming damage
• Precision Attack: Add to attack roll
• Pushing Attack: Push enemy back
• Rally: Give ally temporary HP
• Riposte: Make opportunity attack when missed
• Sweeping Attack: Damage additional creature
• Trip Attack: Knock enemy prone
